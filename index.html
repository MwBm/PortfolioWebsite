Disneyland Data Analysis

Inspired by my experiences at Disneyland, I analyzed the relationship between temperature and wait times using a Kaggle dataset cleaned with Pandas. A Pearson correlation test revealed a weak linear relationship (correlation coefficient: 0.03), likely influenced by the dataset's large size. To address non-normality, I conducted a Kruskal-Wallis H test, which showed statistically significant differences in wait times across temperature categories (H-statistic: 14,358, p < 0.01). These results suggest that temperature impacts wait times at Disneyland.
